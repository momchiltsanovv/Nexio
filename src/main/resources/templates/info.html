Our Story

Nexio is a marketplace built with students in mind. Founded in 2024 by four university students, we saw an opportunity to make student life more convenient and sustainable. In our neighborhoods, we noticed many items that might be discarded as useless‚Äîbut we recognized their value.

Alongside this, we, and many other students, had frustrating experiences with traditional platforms, facing scams, unreliable sellers, and unexpected long-distance trips for disappointing purchases.

Nexio was created to solve these problems by providing a dedicated platform that ensures transactions are safe, verified, and rooted within the student community.

What Makes Us Different

Nexio isn't just another marketplace. Our platform is designed specifically for the student experience. Safety and trust are at the core of what we do; users are verified through their university email, creating a secure environment where students can confidently interact with their peers.
